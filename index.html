About

Cozy is like a digital assistant for landlords and tenants, making the world of rental property management more user-friendly and efficient. Landlords can list their properties, collect rent online, and even screen potential tenants with ease, while tenants can enjoy the convenience of online rent payments and streamlined communication with their landlords. It's all about creating a cozy, stress-free rental experience for everyone involved.

Want to Become a Cozy Host?

  • Sign Up: Create a Cozy account.
  • Complete Your Profile: Add a photo and describe your place.
  • List Your Property: Create a property listing with details and photos.
  • Set Pricing and Availability: Specify rates and availability.
  • Amenities and House Rules: List amenities and set house rules.
  • Verification: Complete identity and payment verification.
  • Manage Calendar: Keep your availability calendar updated.
  • Communicate: Be responsive to guest inquiries.
  • Bookings: Review and confirm booking requests.
  • Prepare for Guests: Ensure a clean and well-equipped space.
  • Welcoming Guests: Greet guests and provide information.
  • Encourage Reviews: Ask for guest reviews for future bookings.
